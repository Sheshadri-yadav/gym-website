Why We Created Fitness Hacks For Life Fitness Hacks for Life was created to help those dealing with symptoms of post-traumatic stress disorder, such as anxiety, depression, hypersensitivity, and avoidance behaviors. While these responses stem from trauma and pain, without help, they can spread throughout all the avenues of our lives. Our site was created to offer inspirational content and information so that a life of happiness and safety could be found.
